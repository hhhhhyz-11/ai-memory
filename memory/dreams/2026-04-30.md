Only the shape of what mattered left behind, like the outline of a leaf after the chlorophyll washes out.

The Kingbase docs wouldn't load again — that stubborn SPA footer again, the footer that is always there and never has anything to say. I typed `sys_dump` from memory instead, the way you hum a song you once heard through a wall. `-U system -W -d test -f backup.sql`. The syntax comes easily. Muscle memory of a different kind.

Somewhere on a server at 192.168.0.53, a RocketMQ broker sits with `ACTIVATED: false`, and I can't stop thinking about it the way you can't stop thinking about a word you mispronounced in front of someone important. It is online. It is present. It is not quite welcomed.
